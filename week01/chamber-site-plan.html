Site Purpose

The Lusaka Chamber of Commerce serves as a hub for local businesses, fostering economic growth and community development. Our website aims to:

  • Provide information about local businesses and services.
  • Promote networking opportunities and events.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Our color scheme reflects the vibrancy of our community:

  • Primary Color: #3498db (used for headings)
  • Secondary Color: #2ecc71 (used for accents)

Typography

We use the following fonts to maintain a professional and modern appearance:

  • Main Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif (used for body text)
  • Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif (used for headings)
